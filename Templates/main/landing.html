Why We Built Trakment

Tired of juggling between planners, diaries, notes, test scores, and syllabus tracking? We felt the same—and that's why we built your all-in-one digital companion, designed by students, for students.

As aspirants ourselves, we understand the challenges of managing intensive study schedules. Trakment was born from our own struggles to stay organized during preparation. We wanted a single platform that could handle all aspects of academic tracking, so we could focus on what really matters - learning.
