How to Use Self Test

1. Select Subject & Chapter

Choose the specific topic you are practicing to keep your analytics organized.

2. Set Question Count

Enter the number of questions in your set (max 180 questions per session).

3. Generate & Start

Tap 'Generate OMR' to create the sheet and automatically start the session timer.

4. Real-life Practice

Solve questions in your actual book/paper, then tap the corresponding option (A-D) on your screen.

5. Track Errors

Check the 'Incorrect' box if you get a question wrong. The app will auto-calculate your marks.

6. Save Progress

Review your summary performance and save to track your growth over time.
